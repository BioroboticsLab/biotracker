First of all, there are the toolbars.

The upper toolbar controls the loading of media, trackers and trajectories.
Additionaly you can switch between the loaded trackers and control the activation of the tracking.

The utility toolbar controls the trajectories and lets you add annotations to each frame of the video.
It offers basic functionalites for an efficient use.

The media toolbar controls the video and shows playback information.
Screenshots and recordings are both possible.

You can move all toolbars to your preferred position or hide them if neccessary.
